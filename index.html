About the Artist

About

Art as the thread
through everything else.

My name is Justin Joonseo Choi. I'm a Public Health and Data Science student at UC Berkeley, currently based in Seoul, Korea. Somewhere between finishing my service in the Korean Air Force and figuring out what comes next. I've been told that's a strange combination of things. I agree.

Art has always been the thread running through everything else. Before consulting or market strategy, I was making digital illustrations about chemistry, trying to explain things like electron uncertainty through color and composition. That instinct to translate the invisible into something felt is still what drives most of my work here. I don't know exactly what kind of artist this makes me. But I've learned to stop worrying about that.
